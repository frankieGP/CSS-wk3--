Hi! Here's an artist we know zilch about, but we're already smitten with his/her weirdness. For sure this voice is pitch-shifted to fudge, in fact a lot of the sonics (save the beats and those hi-hats) sound like they're made out of taffy—stretchy and bendy and thoroughly manipulated—chopped up and triggered to make up the dominant hook.
